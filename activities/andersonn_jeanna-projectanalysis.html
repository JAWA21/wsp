Client: St. Francis Animal Hospital

Project: Website Redesign

Designer: Jeanna Anderson

Document: Project Analysis

Site Intent

The goal of the St. Francis Animal Hospital website redesign is to better reflect the company image and achieve a higher level of usability. Currently they have 12 pages, which will be condensed down to 6 by reorganizing content. The major overhaul will be layout. The homepage will be more comprehensive, to lure the customer in with a couple call to actions. The navigation will become home, about us, services, first visit, links, and reviews.

Competitive Analysis

Based on research conducted, I recommend that St. Francis Animal Hospital position their business as a veterinary hospital that has competitive prices, educated and friendly staff, and available 24/7.

Targeted Audience

Our targeted audience are residential clients. This audience consists mostly of females. These women are single and well educated. Their family consists of their pets. They want a veterinary hospital that they can trust and meet the needs of their pets. Also the services provided by the vet hospital need to be within reach of the targeted audience budget.
